How to sort and filter your transactions on an Android or iOS device

Alistair@MDB

Tap the Transactions icon

Tap the Filter icon to access the Filter Transactions section

Transactions can be sorted by Date, Description and Amount

You can combine sorting styles

Tap the respective radio button to activate editing of the desired sorting style

Transactions can be filtered by Incoming/Outgoing payments, Account(s) and Tags

You can combine filtering styles

Filtering by Tags allows you greater control over what transactions are displayed

Deselect all tags you do not want to include within the results

Tap SAVE when you are happy with your sorting and filtering selections

Your sorted and filtered results will now be shown in the Transactions secion

You can always tell if the Transactions section is showing you filtered results by observing the colour of the Filter icon

If the Filter icon is blue, then the results have been sorted and filtered

To view all transactions without any filters, tap the Filter icon to access the Clear Filters button

That's it, you are now a sorting and filtering pro!
